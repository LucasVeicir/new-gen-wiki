Julian Loki (ジュリアン・ロキ Jurian Roki?) is a prodigious football player known as one of the best players in France, as he is regarded as a supernova in the French league. Julian is also a member of the New Generation World XI, as well as Paris X Gen's team and a forward on the France U-20 team in the PIFA U-20 World Cup. Introduced during Third Selection, as a member of Team World 5, he is initially at Blue Lock to analyze and rate the last string of Blue Lock players. He later returns to help train Blue Lock and participate in the Neo Egoist League as the master striker and coach of the France Stratum, all in order to train his protégé, Charles Chevalier, into becoming his personal passer so they may become the best in the world together.
