Vivian Hugo (ビビアン・ユーゴー Bibian Yūgō?) is a prodigious football player known as one of the best players in France. Hugo is also a member of the New Generation World XI, as well as Arsenaly's team and a center midfielder on the France U-20 team in the PIFA U-20 World Cup. He is regarded as one of the most promising talents in the football world, and unlike other professional strikers, he is only interested in becoming the world's second-best player, operating as a "shadow" to Julien Loki by facilitating the team's success, all to win the World Cup four times and finally surpass Pelé's three-time record.
